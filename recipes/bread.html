Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Put yeast in warm (not boiling but hot) water
  3. Mix flour and salt together in large bowl
  4. Add water/yeast mixture slowly while mixing dough
  5. Knead dough until it comes together
  6. Put dough into cast iron
  7. Bake covered for 20 minutes, uncovered for 10
