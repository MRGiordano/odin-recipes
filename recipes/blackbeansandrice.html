Ingredients

  • Black beans, can or dry
  • onion, chopped
  • garlic, chopped
  • green (or whatever) bell peppers, chopped
  • Adobo seasoning
  • (optional, to taste) some variety of spicy pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. Saute onions in olive oil over medium heat until translucent
  2. Add garlic and green peppers and continue sauteeing until peppers are soft
  3. Add black beans and 2-4 tbsp of Adobo
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes
  5. Serve with rice and cilantro garnish
